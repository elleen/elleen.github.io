Hi, I'm Elleen! I'm a third year student at Northeastern University, studying computer science & interactive media.
Originally from the LA area but got tired of it after living there for almost two decades, so I moved to the other side of the country (Boston) for school.

Some random tidbits: my favorite foods are ice cream (1, 2, 3), tacos, hot cheetos, and watermelon. But my answer to the question, "what could you eat for the rest of your life?" is different - I'd have to say In-N-Out cheeseburgers (the one thing Boston fails to fulfill!) I like exploring new places & am pretty active on both Yelp and Foursquare-now-Swarm. I like to transcribe music, make videos, and make my friends play my transcriptions for said videos. I'm oddly fascinated by weather and transportation. I probably won't ever get sick of snow, even after the 110.6 inch all-time-high we got in the winter of 2014-2015. My favorite social media is Twitter - had it (and deleted) three times starting in 2009 before I decided 140 character intervals were a good amount to share with random strangers : )

This has been weirdly in-depth and I don't really know what I'm supposed to write in a bio. But if you want to talk more, click here!
